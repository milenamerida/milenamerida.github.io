Web Accessibility

On this assignment I learned how important it is to take into consideration the accessibility to the website I am designing. There are different types of impairments such as visual, hearing, mobility and cognitive. Each one has its own specificities and a “solution” to the visitor. It is crucial to learn and know how to include everyone, especially nowadays that we have such an advanced technology, so now that I am learning how to code I am also making a priority to keep accessibility in mind because everyone deserve to have access to the internet.
